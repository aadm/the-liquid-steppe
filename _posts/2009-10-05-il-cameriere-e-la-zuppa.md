Customer says, “Waiter, taste this soup.” 
  
Waiter: "Is it cold?" 
  
Customer: "Just taste the soup." 
  
Waiter: “Didn’t you order the chicken noodle?”  
  
Customer: “Taste . . . the soup!”  
  
Waiter: “O.K., already. Where’s the spoon?”  
  
Customer: “Aha!”
